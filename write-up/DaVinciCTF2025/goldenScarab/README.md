# 🪲 Golden Scarab - EN - Easy

A mysterious scarab discovered in the reserves of the Louvre emits electrical signals that could reveal lost secrets of ancient Egypt. Connect your logic analyzer to the four pins : PB10, PB11, PA6, and PA7 on the STM32 side to decode the hidden message. The Egyptian priests used two distinct encoding methods: Horus' simple protocol (UART) and Anubis' complex encoding (Manchester).
